OVERVIEW
===========================================================

The Aryan POC Liquidity Model (APLM) is an intraday trading
framework designed specifically for XAUUSD.

The strategy combines:

1. Fixed Range Volume Profile
2. Point of Control (POC)
3. Smart Money Concepts (SMC)
4. Liquidity Theory
5. Session Analysis

The main objective is to identify high-probability directional
moves during the New York Session.

The strategy is built on the belief that the Point of Control
acts as a liquidity magnet and institutional reference level.

===========================================================
MARKET THEORY
===========================================================

Markets move from liquidity to liquidity.

Retail traders often focus on indicators.

Institutions focus on:

- Liquidity
- Volume
- Order Flow
- Position Accumulation
- Fair Value Areas

The market typically follows this sequence:

1. Asian Session Creates Liquidity
2. London Session Expands Liquidity
3. New York Session Releases Liquidity

This creates directional opportunities.

===========================================================
POINT OF CONTROL (POC)
===========================================================

POC = Price level where maximum volume occurred.

This is the most important level in the strategy.

Why?

Because institutions have executed the largest amount
of orders at this level.

The POC acts as:

- Fair Value
- Liquidity Magnet
- Institutional Reference Price

Price frequently reacts around POC.

===========================================================
DAILY BIAS
===========================================================

BULLISH BIAS

Conditions:

1. Previous downside liquidity has already been taken.

2. Selling pressure appears exhausted.

3. Market begins seeking upside liquidity.

4. Internal liquidity exists above current price.

Result:

Look for buys only.

-----------------------------------------------------------

BEARISH BIAS

Conditions:

1. Previous upside liquidity has already been taken.

2. Buying pressure appears exhausted.

3. Market begins seeking downside liquidity.

4. Internal liquidity exists below current price.

Result:

Look for sells only.

===========================================================
INTERNAL LIQUIDITY
===========================================================

Internal Liquidity means:

Smaller liquidity structures that contribute to
the larger market objective.

Examples:

- Equal Highs
- Equal Lows
- Small Swing Highs
- Small Swing Lows
- Consolidation Highs
- Consolidation Lows

These become future targets.
